description: >
    Vim Week was a series of 3 lectures I held at the end of the Fall 2024
    semester. The content is designed to teach the basics of Vim-style navigation
    and editing and to show a few examples of what’s possible with more advanced
    Neovim setups, e.g. getting language-intelligent features right in the editor.

    It’s aimed at those taking Purdue’s computer science classes involving C
    programming, namely CS 240, CS 250, CS 252, and CS 354.

    Over 75 students attended, and at least 58 went on to use my Neovim
    installation on the Purdue CS servers.
